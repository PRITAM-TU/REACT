Rock Paper Scissors Rules

Basic Rules

  • Players simultaneously choose one of three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors
  • The game has three possible outcomes: win, lose, or draw
  • Standard gameplay is best of 3 or best of 5 rounds

Winning Combinations

✊ Rock
beats
✌️ Scissors
✋ Paper
beats
✊ Rock
✌️ Scissors
beats
✋ Paper

Additional Rules

  • If both players choose the same option, it's a tie and the round is replayed
  • Players must make their choice within the time limit (typically 3 seconds)
  • Some variations include Lizard and Spock for more complexity

How to Play

  1. Players stand facing each other
  2. On the count of three, players simultaneously form one of the three shapes
  3. Determine the winner based on the combinations above
  4. The first player to win the majority of rounds (2/3 or 3/5) wins the game
